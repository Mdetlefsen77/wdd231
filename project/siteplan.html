Travel Tales

This name was chosen to reflect the storytelling nature of travel experiences.

Purpose of the Site

To inspire and inform travelers by providing recommendations for destinations, travel tips, and a curated gallery of travel images.

Scenarios

  • What are the top destinations for adventure travel?
  • How can I get reliable travel tips for solo travelers?
  • What destinations are ideal for a honeymoon?
  • What are the best tips for traveling on a budget?
  • What should I pack for a trip to Iceland in winter?
  • How can I find reliable local guides for tours in Asia?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #3498DB (Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, and links.

Secondary Color: #F5F5DC (Beige) - Used as the background color for most sections.

Accent Color: #F39C12 (Orange) - Used for call-to-action buttons and important highlights.

Typography

Header Font: Merriweather

Body Font: Roboto
